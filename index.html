Cold Case Pre-Investigation Diagnostic

20 questions · 8 governance conditions · 5 minutes. Adapted from the HSRL Structural Governance Framework for cold case investigation governance. Answer each question for the case under evaluation before assigning investigative resources.

This diagnostic tests whether the structural preconditions for a resolvable cold case investigation are present. Absence of a condition does not indicate investigator failure. It indicates that the investigation lacks the structural capacity to be governed — meaning errors cannot be caught, leads cannot be tracked, and accountability cannot survive personnel transitions.

In 147 of 147 permanently unresolved cold cases in the HSRL dataset, at least one of these eight conditions was absent or degraded at T1. In all 93 resolved cases, governance scores were above 0.6 and no condition was fully absent.
